Your Task

Your task is to visualize the comparison of budgets.

The module floatCharts.budget contains a service for retrieving budget data.

The costType may be selectable in the frontend (i.e. show me the numbers/charts for NETNET), but all amounts must have the same costType, (the four different costTypes must not be mixed).

Additionally we want to see the progression of the budget over 2 year (i.e. compare 2016 with 2017). Did we spend in 2017 less than in 2016, then use a red/signal color or find another way to display this situation in a suitable chart/manner)

How the data should be presented is up to you. Also feel free to install third-party libs as you need and create all the angular components/directives, services, filters and other stuff you need.

Commit your work via git (maybe like in a normal workflow and not all-in-one) and reach us when you are finished.
