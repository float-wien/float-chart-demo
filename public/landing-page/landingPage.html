Your Task

Your task is to visualize the comparison of budgets.

The module floatCharts.budget contains a service for retrieving budget data. The app should visualize the current years budget and a comparison with last year.

How the data should be presented is up to you. Also feel free to install third-party libs as you need and create all the angular components/directives, services, filters and other stuff you need.

Commit your work via git (maybe like in a normal workflow and not all-in-one) and reach us when you are finished.
